# lilleskog-voicemaster-3000
Local voice mastering app. Drop a recording, pick your scenario, get broadcast-ready audio — no audio engineering degree required.

Lilleskog Voicemaster 3000 takes a raw voice recording and makes it sound like it was produced. Drag in a file, tell it how it was recorded, and get a mastered WAV back in under a minute.

Eight context-aware presets cover the real scenarios people actually record in — studio mic, headset, Zoom call, phone, outdoor, interview, group discussion, untreated room — each one tuning a full effects chain: noise reduction, high-pass filter, parametric EQ, compression, limiting, and loudness normalisation to −16 LUFS (the podcast and streaming standard). The presets aren't just labels. Each one explains what it does and why, in plain English.

Runs entirely on your machine. No cloud, no subscription, no account. Supports WAV, FLAC, MP3, AIFF, M4A, and OGG. Ships with before/after spectrograms and a real-time processing log with full technical details for the curious.
